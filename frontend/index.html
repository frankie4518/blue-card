FAQ

Do I have to move my existing domain? +
No. We can either transfer it to Blue‑Card or manage it in place, depending on what’s safest.
What’s the difference between Helping Hand and self‑managed? +
Helping Hand means we manage DNS, SSL and routing for you. Self‑managed means you control DNS, but still buy and renew through Blue‑Card.
Can you migrate my existing website? +
Yes. We’ll agree a safe migration window and handle the technical steps for you.
